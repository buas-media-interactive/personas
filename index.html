üéØ Your Task (Product Coordinator Perspective)

For your assigned persona, answer these questions:

  1. Context of Use: When, where, and how will they interact with this product? What's happening around them?
  2. Tasks & Goals: What specific actions do they need to accomplish? What does "success" look like in the moment of use?
  3. Pain Points & Frustrations: What could go wrong during their experience? What would make them quit or get annoyed?
  4. Technical Considerations: What devices, connectivity, accessibility needs, or technical comfort level matters?
  5. Behavioral Patterns: How do they actually interact with similar products? Any habits or shortcuts they rely on?
Remember: Marketing tells you who they are and why they'd choose you. Product tells you how to serve them well once they're using your product.
